Emotional Journey by Stage
1. Awareness
Neutral / Curious
Sees kiosk in mall, Instagram post, or friend recommendation. Brand unknown but intriguing. Key tactic: Eye-catching kiosk design, free samples, micro-influencer seeding (50/outlet).
+20
2. Consideration
Intrigued & positive
Checks Google Maps, reads 4.8★ reviews, compares to KFC. Leans toward trying. Pain point: Menu complexity causes choice paralysis. Fix: Highlight "Most Popular" hero item clearly.
+30
3. Decision
⚠️ Anxious
Stands at kiosk, stares at 12 items, second-guesses price vs KFC. Fix: Make Fish Popcorn 3x larger on board, staff script "95% of first-timers start here!", risk reversal "Love it or money back!"
-10
4. First Bite ✨
Peak Delight!
"Wow, this is SO GOOD!" Takes photo, tells 3–5 friends. Critical action: Send SMS within 2 hrs, receipt coupon "₹50 off in 7 days" to trigger Visit 2. Get that Google review.
+85
5. Engagement
Satisfied, building trust
Visits 2–5. Has a "usual order". Starts bringing others. Risk: Menu fatigue after 3–4 visits. Fix: Rotate 2–3 Limited Time items monthly, staff recognition ("Welcome back!"), surprise free drink after Visit 3.
+60
6. Loyalty
Evangelist!
FishBae Pass subscriber (₹999/mo). Visits 2–3x/week. Brings new people, posts regularly. Tactics: "FishBae Insiders" community, quarterly founder dinner for top 50, referral rewards (₹500 per 3 referrals).
+75
7. Churn
Disappointed / Indifferent
Top reasons: Inconsistent quality (32%), menu fatigue (25%), poor service (18%), price (15%). Win-back: Day 14 SMS ₹50 off → Day 30 survey → Day 45 "50% off — last chance". Founder calls high-value churners personally.
-30

Industry Trends & Intelligence
5 macro forces + 7 micro trends — all rated for FishBae impact
5 Macro Trends (Global Forces)
Protein Substitution Megatrend
9/10
India seafood consumption up 50% in 5 years. Fish protein searches +340% YoY. WHO recommends 2x fish/week — only 25% of Indians comply currently. Massive tailwind.
Premiumization of Fast Food
8/10
Premium QSR (₹350+) growing at 24% CAGR vs 14% overall. Middle class expanding to 600M by 2030. Millennials pay 40% premium for "authentic + healthy".
Experience Economy & Social Media
8/10
83% of Gen Z photograph food before eating. UGC drives 4.2x more engagement than brand content. Fish Popcorn bucket = Instagram gold.
Delivery & Ghost Kitchen
7/10
Dine-in rebounding +28% YoY. 72% say delivered fish is "never as good." FishBae's mall kiosk model is RIGHT. Delivery = off-peak fill, not core.
Food Safety Regulations
6/10
FSSAI inspections up 3x since 2023. 40% non-compliance penalties. Build cold chain documentation + lab testing from Day 1. Compliance = moat vs street vendors.
